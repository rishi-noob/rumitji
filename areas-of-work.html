Areas of Work

Discover my multifaceted work at the intersection of environment, peace, and space exploration.

Environment

Environment

My environmental work journey began at around 12 when I started collecting discarded paper and aluminium foil from my school classroom. Growing up in a farming background, my love for trees and nature has always driven me to work for the planet as a responsibility rather than just a job or hobby.

Over the years, I have collaborated with hundreds of organizations in this field. I am actively involved with Tears of the Earth, which I co-founded in 2010. Additionally, I contribute to Earth Day Network, where I manage youth affairs in India, and I volunteer and advise on environmental engagements at the American Center, New Delhi.

Peacebuilding

Peacebuilding

My involvement in peacebuilding began in 2018 when I started volunteering for organizations such as the Global Peace Foundation, National Youth Project by Dr. S.N Subba Rao, and others. However, my dedication to this cause deepened significantly during the Delhi Riots in 2020.

I further contributed by assisting in the Declaration of the Global Peace Leadership Conference, Indo-Pacific, and advocating for peace in several other countries. I have also been involved in interfaith leadership and community-driven peacebuilding. Currently, I moderate the Young Changemakers Dialogue and collaborate with several embassies on initiatives for peacebuilding intersections.

Performative Arts

Performative Arts

I strongly believe in the transformative power of performative arts. Music, dance, theatre, and art are integral to every movement, campaign, or commemoration. I am currently the vocalist in the Virah Band, where we create and perform songs focusing on climate justice, environmental conservation, unity, and more.

Our performances have taken us to prestigious venues such as C20 events, Banaras Hindu University, BITS Pilani, Palam Airforce Station, Local Conference of Youth (LCOY) Kerala, Pragati Maidan, and many others. I have personally performed at International Events including Territory of Meaning, Moscow.

Space Exploration

Space Exploration

I've always been fascinated by science, especially the connection between space exploration and environmental conservation. My interest in this grew after engaging with the iHub at the U.S Embassy in Delhi.

I now lead the Future Forward Concept, focusing on space exploration's intersection with peace and the environment. I am a member of OpenSpace International for policy and outreach, and involved with the Space Generation Advisory Council (SGAC).

People & Youth

People & Youth

In recent years, I have engaged with various audiences in diverse ways, particularly in managing youth groups and interacting with larger crowds. I have had the opportunity to address tens of thousands of individuals, including moderating high-level sessions with government officials on national and international platforms.

My engagement extends to continuous shows, such as a 53-day consecutive Instagram Live session and currently hosting a 54-week video podcast series on "Planet vs. Plastics".

Currently, I am hosting 5 Seconds for Earth — a 5-second show for delivering tips to become a true environmentalist.
